What We Use to Learn

Creative Arts & Literature

Every child is an artist. The problem is how to remain an artist once we grow up.

Pablo Picasso

Through art, literature and music, our students learn to think critically, to empathize with others and to express themselves in creative ways.

Learn More

Games & Sports

You are never really playing an opponent. You are playing yourself, your own highest standards, and when you reach your limits, that is real joy

Arthur Ashe

Students get to strengthen both their bodies and minds while still having fun by learning team-work, psychology, strategy and at the same time developing respect, self-discipline, their self-esteem, determination and grit.

Learn More

Mathematics

Go down deep enough into anything and you will find mathematics.

Dean Schlicter

Math is a universal language. By studying mathematics, our students are able to appreciate and communicate the secrets of the universe in a logical manner.

Learn More

Natural Sciences

The cosmos is within us. We are made of star-stuff. We are a way for the universe to know itself

Carl Sagan

At Hera, we encourage our students to be fiercely curious about their natural environment, to apply the scientific process to discover the laws that govern nature for themselves. It is our hope that our students will experience the joys and satisfaction of knowing (or pursuing knowledge of) “how stuff works”, how everything is interconnected and how this reflects upon their own existence.

Learn More

Programming & Robotics

The reasonable man adapts himself to the world: the unreasonable one persists in trying to adapt the world to himself. Therefore all progress depends on the unreasonable man.

George Bernard Shaw, Man and Superman

We nurture creativity and self-confidence in our students by teaching them problem-solving and practical programming skills (languages) and encouraging them to collaborate and apply these skills to invent and manipulate systems and data to solve everyday problems within their environment.
